The Mission

Imagine learning one new thing every day. Doesn't seem like a lot, right? But imagine if you could do that every day for a year, or even longer. You'd have a wealth of knowledge at your fingertips, and the best part is, it doesn't have to be overwhelming.

The goal of this website and my YouTube channel is to make learning STEM subjects accessible and enjoyable for everyone. I believe that with the right approach, anyone can grasp complex concepts without feeling lost or intimidated.

What's Here?

On this site, you will find links to all my videos, playlists, and resources. Additionally, you will have access to other tools like an AI study companion, review quizzes, and a means of directly contacting me for video suggestions/inquiries.
